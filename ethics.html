Welcome to the Ethics page of my portfolio.

As a student and aspiring computing professional, I am committed to upholding the highest standards of ethical conduct in all my work. The ACM Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct serves as a guiding framework for my actions and decisions.

General Ethical Principles

As a student and aspiring computing professional, I am committed to contributing to society and human well-being, recognizing that all people are stakeholders in computing. My work aims to improve the quality of life for all individuals, promoting fundamental human rights and protecting each person's right to autonomy. I strive to minimize negative consequences of computing, including threats to health, safety, personal security, and privacy, and prioritize the needs of those less advantaged when interests conflict. Transparency and accountability are core values in my work, and I am dedicated to being honest about my capabilities, limitations, and the potential impacts of my projects. Respecting the privacy of individuals and organizations is fundamental to my ethical practice, ensuring that data is collected, used, and stored responsibly. Additionally, I honor the confidentiality of information entrusted to me, whether it pertains to individuals, organizations, or projects, as maintaining trust is essential in all professional relationships.

Professional Responsibilities

As a dedicated student and aspiring computing professional, I strive for high-quality work that meets the standards of the computing profession, continuously learning and improving to achieve excellence. I commit to ongoing education and skill development to stay current with advancements in the field, ensuring my work remains relevant and effective. Adhering to all applicable laws and regulations is a critical component of my ethical conduct. I welcome constructive feedback and peer review, recognizing that collaboration and critique are vital for growth and improvement. Additionally, I provide honest and thorough evaluations of systems, projects, and technologies, considering their broader impacts on society and the environment.

Leadership Responsibilities

In any leadership role, I am committed to promoting ethical conduct among my peers and team members, leading by example to foster an ethical culture. I manage resources and personnel with integrity, ensuring that decisions are made in the best interest of all stakeholders. Additionally, I strive to enhance public understanding of computing and its impacts, advocating for informed and responsible use of technology. I respect the boundaries of access to computing resources, ensuring that all actions are authorized and legitimate.
